About ARC Physical Therapy

Friendly, focused care built around your goals.

ARC Physical Therapy is dedicated to one-on-one patient care, customized treatment plans, and helping people return to everyday activities with confidence. The clinic focuses on advanced orthopedic physical therapy services designed to improve comfort, movement, strength, and quality of life.

From the first visit through the final progress check, ARC keeps treatment personal, clear, and goal-based. Patients receive hands-on support, education, and home strategies that help recovery continue outside the clinic.
